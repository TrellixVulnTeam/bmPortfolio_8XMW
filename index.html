Focus and Passions

I am multimedia journalist with experience creating digital content and short docu-series. In my work I am committed to introducing sociological concepts into my productions, being BIPOC I share a similar worldview and life experience with so many others that may not get the same opportunity to speak and share the realities of their lives. My work and productions allows me to bridge that gap and let our experiences be known to the world.
